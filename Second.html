Call of Duty:Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is a free-to-play shooter game developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Activision for Android and iOS
It was released on October 1, 2019, where it was one of the largest mobile game launches in history, generating over US$480 million with 270 million downloads
within a year. Call of Duty: Mobile was published in other regions by Garena, Tencent Games, VNG Games and TiMi Studio Group.
